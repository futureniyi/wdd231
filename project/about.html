Our Story

Relationship Battery was created to help newly married couples in Nigeria strengthen their partnership through confidential, expert counseling.

We know every relationship is unique, and every couple deserves the tools to thrive.

Our team of licensed counselors are passionate about guiding you to deeper connection and understanding—all in a private, online environment.

What We Stand For

  • Privacy: Your information is safe, always.
  • Support: Real help, whenever you need it.
  • Growth: Every couple can build a stronger, happier relationship.
  • Accessibility: Expert counseling is just a click away, anywhere in Nigeria.

Meet Our Therapists

Our therapists are passionate, highly trained, and committed to helping couples build stronger, happier relationships. Every member of our team is certified, experienced, and brings warmth and understanding to every session.

Why trust us? We work only with licensed professionals who are experts in marriage counseling, relationship psychology, and emotional wellness. With years of experience and a deep respect for your privacy, our therapists guide you with empathy and real insight—every step of the way.
