Olaniyi Bello - Site Plan

Site Name: RelationshipBattery

Relationship Battery is a modern, friendly brand that reflects the idea of regularly “checking in” on your relationship’s well-being. It’s simple, memorable, and signals the supportive, uplifting nature of the counseling services offered. The domain, relationshipbattery.com, is easy to remember and communicates the site’s purpose at a glance.

Site Purpose

This website is designed to support newly married couples by providing access to counseling resources. Couples can fill out a brief, confidential questionnaire, learn more about the counseling process, and reach out for support. The site emphasizes privacy, honesty, and a safe environment for couples seeking to strengthen their marriage.

Scenarios

  • How do we start with marriage counseling as a new couple?
  • What will happen during and after we fill out the questionnaire?
  • How do we ask questions or get help if we’re stuck?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #483D8B (Indigo/Purple) – Used for main backgrounds, header, and footer.
  • Secondary Color: #E6E6FA (Lavender) – Used for headings and link hover/active states.
  • Highlight Color: #98FB98 (Light Green) – Used for links, highlighted text, and “last updated” info.
  • Text Color: #FFFFFF (White) – Used for main text on dark backgrounds.
  • Color Scheme

Typography

Clear, inviting fonts are used to create a modern and approachable feeling. Headings and buttons use Montserrat for a friendly, bold look. Lato is applied to body text for readability and openness. PT Serif is used for quotes or to highlight special content, adding a touch of elegance where needed.

  • Montserrat – Used for headings and buttons
  • Lato – Used for body text and paragraphs
  • PT Serif – Used for quotes or special highlights (optional)
