Redeemer's 2007 Alumni Awards - Site Plan

Site Name: Redeemer's 2007 Alumni Awards

Redeemer's 2007 Alumni Awards is a clear, legacy-focused name that honors the graduating class of 2007. It emphasizes community involvement, student recognition, and ongoing alumni engagement.

Site Purpose

This website will celebrate academic and leadership achievements by providing an organized platform to view alumni-sponsored awards, eligibility requirements, and participation options. It promotes legacy, generosity, and connection among the class of 2007.

Scenarios

  • How can alumni contribute or sponsor a new award?
  • What awards are available for current students or fellow alumni?
  • How do I join the alumni network or donate to existing funds?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #14447e (Deep Indigo) – Used for main backgrounds, header, and footer.
  • Secondary Color: #048004 (Vibrant Green) – Used for links, buttons, and active states.
  • Highlight Color: #98FB98 (Pale Green) – Used for highlights, notifications, or success messages.
  • Border Color: #F4F4F4 (Soft Gray) – Used for borders, dividers, and light background elements.
  • Color Scheme

Typography

Clear, inviting fonts are used to create a modern and approachable feeling. Headings and buttons use Montserrat for a friendly, bold look. Lato is applied to body text for readability and openness. PT Serif is used for quotes or to highlight special content, adding a touch of elegance where needed.

  • Montserrat – Used for headings and buttons
  • Lato – Used for body text and paragraphs
  • PT Serif – Used for quotes or special highlights (optional)
