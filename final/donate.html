Donation Process

Step 1

Please send your donations to our dedicated bank account below:

  • Account Name: RHS 007 Alumni

  • Account Number: 1234567890

Step 2

Email your transaction receipt to donations@rhs007.com.ng for confirmation.

Upon confirmation, you will receive a unique donation code.
This code will act as your public name, appearing next to your donation amount on our publicly displayed donor list.

For example: Zion – ₦10,000

Step 3

After 24 hours, check the Donors list below to see your donation code and amount displayed.
