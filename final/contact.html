Frequently Asked Questions

What is the RHS 2007 Alumni Bond Set?

The RHS 2007 Alumni Bond Set is the official graduating set of Redeemer's High School Senior Secondary School, class of 2007. We foster lifelong connections, give back to the school community, and celebrate excellence.

Who can join the RHS 2007 Alumni Association?

Graduates from the 2007 Senior Secondary School class of RHS, as well as supporters and stakeholders of the alumni initiatives.

What are the main goals of the RHS 2007 Alumni?

To support annual prize awards for current students and teachers, encourage community service, organize events, and maintain a network of past students.

How can I support RHS 2007 Alumni initiatives?

You can make donations via the dedicated bank account, email your transaction receipt to donations@rhs007.com.ng, and receive a unique donation code visible on the donor list.

What happens after I make a donation?

After confirmation, you will receive a unique donation code to appear publicly next to your contribution, and your support helps fund student and teacher awards.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous or request that your name not appear on the public donor list.

What kinds of awards does the RHS 2007 Alumni support?

Awards include cash prizes, certificates, books, and plaques recognizing academic excellence and dedicated service from students and teachers.

How are award winners selected?

Students are nominated by their school principals based on academic performance, while teachers are selected through alumni votes verified by the organizing committee.

Are the awards only for the 2007 graduating set?

While the current initiative is led by the 2007 set, the aim is to expand and encourage participation across all graduating classes.

How can I stay updated on alumni events and news?

Follow the RHS 2007 Alumni website, social media pages, and newsletters for updates on events like prize ceremonies, birthday shoutouts, and alumni achievements.

Can I contribute ideas or volunteer for alumni projects?

Yes! Contact the alumni committee via the official contact page to express interest in volunteering or contributing ideas.
