2025 Prize Ceremony Highlights

The 2025 Prize Ceremony was a memorable day filled with joy and pride as our best graduating students and dedicated teachers were honored by the RHS 007 Alumni community.

Students across Junior and Senior schools were recognized for academic excellence, leadership, and community service. Our teacher legacy awards celebrated those who have dedicated over a decade to shaping young minds.

The ceremony featured speeches from distinguished alumni and heartfelt gratitude from award recipients, fostering inspiration for future generations.

Highlights included:

  • Presentation of cash prizes, plaques, and books selected by the alumni.
  • Special performance by the school choir.
  • Networking session with alumni and community leaders.

We thank all contributors and look forward to next year’s event!

Birthday Celebrations

In July, we celebrated the birthdays of our wonderful RHS 007 alumni community members. This month’s birthday shoutouts included personalized messages, spotlight stories, and a special digital card shared across social media.

We highlighted the achievements and milestones of those born in July and encouraged everyone to connect and celebrate together.

Thank you for being part of this vibrant community! Keep an eye on our socials for upcoming birthday celebrations and opportunities to participate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the RHS 2007 Alumni Bond Set?

The RHS 2007 Alumni Bond Set is the official graduating class of Redeemer's High School Senior Secondary School, class of 2007. We foster lifelong connections, support our alma mater, and celebrate excellence through community service and annual awards.

How can I support RHS 2007 Alumni initiatives?

You can support us by donating to the dedicated bank account listed on our Donate page and emailing your transaction receipt to donations@rhs007.com.ng. After confirmation, you will receive a unique donation code to appear on the public donor list.

Who selects the award winners?

Student award winners are nominated by school principals based on academic performance. Teacher awards are selected through alumni voting and verified by the organizing committee. Visit the Awards page for more details.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes, you may choose to donate anonymously or request that your name not appear on the public donor list. Learn more on our Donate page.

How can I contact the RHS 2007 Alumni Committee?

You can email us at hello@rhs007.com.ng or use the contact form on our Contact page.

Where is the RHS 2007 Alumni Committee located?

The committee is based at Redeemer's High School, Redemption Camp, Ogun State, Nigeria.

How are award winners recognized?

Winners receive cash prizes, certificates, books, and plaques to celebrate academic excellence and dedicated service. See examples on the Awards page.
